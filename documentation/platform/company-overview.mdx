Brale is a regulated stablecoin issuer built by a high-integrity team with deep payments, banking, and infrastructure experience. We combine a proprietary issuance/orchestration stack with a proprietary regulatory stack so you can launch and scale stablecoin-enabled products with confidence.

## Why teams choose Brale

- **Regulated foundation:** Operates as a supervised, compliance-first issuer with robust KYC/KYB, AML, and treasury controls.
- **High-integrity team:** Veterans from payments, banking, and web3 infrastructure who prioritize safety, resilience, and clear communication.
- **Proprietary tech stack:** End-to-end platform for issuance, custody, on/off-ramps, swaps, payouts, and treasury operations—exposed via clean, well-documented APIs.
- **Proprietary regulatory stack:** Embedded compliance workflows (KYC/KYB, sanctions screening, address risk checks, auditability) to keep you within policy while you ship.
- **Performance and reliability:** Built for uptime, observability, and predictable latency across auth, transfers, and data endpoints.
- **Transparent backing:** Stablecoins are fully reserved with clear proof and controls designed for institutional-grade trust.

## What you can build

- **Issue and manage stablecoins:** Launch branded stablecoins backed by Brale’s reserves and controls.
- **Move value everywhere:** Onramp/offramp, swap across supported chains and stablecoins, automate payouts, and manage custody for your customers.
- **Operate with confidence:** Idempotent, well-instrumented APIs; clear error surfaces; and support that engages with your engineers, not just tickets.

## How we support you

- **API-first:** OAuth2 client credentials, OpenAPI-powered reference, and Postman-friendly endpoints.
- **Compliance built in:** KYC/KYB, sanctions, address risk checks, transaction monitoring, audit trails.
- **Partnership model:** Direct access to Brale engineers for integration guidance, architecture reviews, and go-live readiness.
