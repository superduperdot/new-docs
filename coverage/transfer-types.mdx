title: 'Transfer Types'
description: 'Supported transfer rails for moving value on/off chain.'
---

Brale supports these transfer types for funding and payouts. Use them in `transfer_type` fields on addresses and transfers. Source: [TransferType.json](https://github.com/Brale-xyz/commons/blob/main/Resources/TransferType.json).

## Onchain

- ethereum
- base
- solana
- canton
- stellar
- polygon
- avalanche
- optimism
- arbitrum
- hedera
- celo
- kusama
- spark
- xrp_ledger

## Offchain

- wire
- ach-debit
- ach-credit
- same-day-ach-credit
- rtp-credit

If you need additional rails, contact support@brale.xyz.
