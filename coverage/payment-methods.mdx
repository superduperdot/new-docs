  Brale enables deposits and withdraws into and out of U.S. bank accounts.

## United States

Supported methods include:

- Wire (Domestic only)
- ACH Credit (standard and same day)
- ACH Debit (standard and same day)
- RTP

We continually bring on new partners to support more payment methods. Please reachout to us at support@brale.xyz if you'd like to see additional methods supported.
